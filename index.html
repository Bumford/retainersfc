About the Club

Founded in 1953, Retainers FC has been a proud member of the Bristol Downs League ever since. Retainers FC are two times title winners. We currently run two teams competing in Division 1 and Division 3. In the 24/25 season, both teams secured impressive 4th-place finishes.

We're a friendly, inclusive and diverse club always welcoming new players. If you're interested in regular 11-a-side Saturday football on the Downs, get in touch!

We play weekly at the Downs and train at Orchard School in North Bristol 7pm on Tuesdays during the season and at 6pm on Tuesdays at the Downs during the off season.
